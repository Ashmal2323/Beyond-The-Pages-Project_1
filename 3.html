Famous Books

"Through books, we live a thousand lives and feel every emotion in the world"

To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Description: A profound novel that explores racial injustice in the Deep South through the eyes of a young girl, Scout Finch. It's a timeless tale of morality, compassion, and courage.

1984

Author: George Orwell

Description: A dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society under constant surveillance. It delves into themes of power, control, and freedom of thought.

Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Description: A classic romance novel that examines themes of love, social class, and personal growth through the story of Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy.

The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Description: A critique of the American Dream, this novel explores wealth, love, and betrayal in the roaring 1920s through the mysterious Jay Gatsby.

War and Peace

Author: Leo Tolstoy

Description: It is a historical novel set during the Napoleonic Wars, focusing on Russian aristocratic families. It explores themes of love, war, and the human condition.

Peer-e-Kamil

Author: Umera Ahmed

Description: A spiritual and emotional journey of love, faith, and redemption, following the transformative lives of Imama and Salar.

Zaviya

Author: Ashfaq Ahmed

Description: A collection of essays and stories filled with wisdom, philosophy, and reflections on life and relationships.

Raja Gidh

Author: Bano Qudsia

Description: A thought-provoking novel exploring the ethical and spiritual dilemmas of human existence through allegory and philosophy.

Humsafar

Author: Farhat Ishtiaq

Description: A romantic and emotional story of love, trust, and misunderstandings, beautifully portraying the bond between Khirad and Ashar.

Shahab Nama

Author: Qudrat Ullah Shahab

Description: An autobiographical masterpiece blending personal experiences with spiritual and political reflections on Pakistan.
